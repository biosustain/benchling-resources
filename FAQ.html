How can I provide a colleague with access to Benchling?

First-time access is granted by HR. Contact Biosustain HR to request access for your collegue.

How can I provide a colleague with access to my data?

Make sure they are included in the collaborators of your Benchling project.

  • If they are part of your team, add them in the Benchling Team of your research group and add the Team as collaborator in your project.

  • If they are not part of your team, add them directly as collaborator in your project.
